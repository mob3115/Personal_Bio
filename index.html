About Me

Where technology
meets purpose.

I'm Mark O'Brien, Director of Customer Technology and Product Innovation at The Cleveland Foundation — one of the world's first and largest community foundations. My work sits at the intersection of organizational strategy, digital products, and financial expertise that promote human flourishing.

Beyond the foundation, I run Customer X Lab, a consulting practice dedicated to helping organizations reimagine their customer experience from the inside out. I'm also the author of Fueling Customer Delight — a practical guide to building cultures where extraordinary service isn't the exception, it's the standard — along with four additional ebooks exploring CX, leadership, and growth.

I believe the best technology is invisible. It quietly removes friction, amplifies human connection, and makes complex things feel simple. That conviction drives everything I build.

What Drives Me

Passion &
Philosophy

Technology in the Service of Humans

I believe technology only earns its place when it makes someone's life genuinely better. Not more efficient for the business — better for the person. That distinction is everything. Empathy isn't a soft skill; it's the most precise diagnostic tool we have for uncovering what humans actually need — before they can articulate it themselves.

I believe our work should build up ourselves and others — that commerce and community aren't in tension, they're in service of each other. Business is one of the most powerful vehicles for human flourishing we've ever invented, and when we use it that way — with intention, with empathy, and with others in mind — it has the power to make the world genuinely better.
